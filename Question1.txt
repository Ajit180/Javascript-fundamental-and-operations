Q1. Explain the role of operators in JavaScript. Why are they essential in programming?

Solutions:

     # The Role of Operators in JavaScript

Operators in JavaScript are symbols or keywords used to perform operations on variables and values. They are essential in programming because they:

1. Facilitate Computations: Enable arithmetic operations and data manipulations.
2. Control Logic Flow: Allow for decision-making and comparisons.
3. Simplify Code: Provide shorthand methods for common tasks.
4. Enable Complex Expressions: Combine operations to create powerful expressions.

In essence, operators are fundamental tools that allow developers to perform a wide range of tasks efficiently, from basic arithmetic to complex logical operations.
